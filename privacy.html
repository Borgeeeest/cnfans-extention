Privacy Policy – CNFans Warning Borgfinds

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information, browsing history, or user data.

All functionality is executed locally in the browser, and the extension only modifies content on the CNFans website to improve user experience (such as hiding popups and appending affiliate links).

No data is sent to any server, and no tracking is performed.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact the developer.
